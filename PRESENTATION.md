# ORACLE 
### School Fee Management System
*Project Presentation*

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
- Schools and educational organizations struggle with manual fee tracking and receipt generation.
- Parents face difficulties tracking due dates, past payments, and avoiding late fees.
- Lack of centralized reporting leads to discrepancies between cash/cheque collections and actual bank deposits.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution
**ORACLE** is a comprehensive, centralized School Fee Management System that bridges the gap between administrators, organizations, and parents/students.
- Automates fee tracking and receipt generation.
- Provides specialized portals for Admins, Organizations, and Parents.
- Incorporates a secure verification workflow for manual payments.

---

## ✨ Key Features
- **Automated fee tracking:** Schedules, instalments and grace periods handled automatically with due-date reminders.
- **Digital receipts & history:** Instant receipt generation, searchable payment history, and exportable audit trails for accounting.
- **Role-based access:** Separate admin and staff privileges to protect financial data and control verification workflows.
- **Real-time reporting:** Dashboards and interactive charts for collections, outstanding balances and trend analysis.
- **Secure cloud sync:** Encrypted storage, daily backups and cross-device consistency minimise data loss risk.

---

## 🏗️ System Architecture
- **Frontend Layer:** Flutter (Cross-platform Web/Mobile)
- **State Management:** Riverpod for reactive, predictable state transitions.
- **Routing:** GoRouter for robust navigation and deep linking.
- **Backend Layer:** Supabase (PostgreSQL Database, Authentication).

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
- **Framework:** Flutter & Dart
- **Backend:** Supabase
- **State Management:** Riverpod
- **UI & Visualization:** Google Fonts, FL Chart, Flutter Animate
- **Routing:** GoRouter

---

## 🚀 Future Scope
- **Payment Gateway Integration:** Direct integrations with Stripe/Razorpay for automated online processing.
- **Automated Notifications:** SMS and Email reminders for upcoming due dates and late fees.
- **Mobile App Release:** Publishing natively to the App Store and Google Play.
- **Advanced Reporting:** Exportable PDF/Excel compliance reports for financial auditing.
